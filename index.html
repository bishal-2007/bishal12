About Us

BARKASS was founded in 2024 by **Bishal Upadhyay**, who envisioned creating a premium juice brand that prioritizes health, innovation, and taste. The journey began with the mission to bring a refreshing alternative to the overly processed beverages dominating the market. Inspired by nature and driven by the desire to craft something unique, Bishal Upadhyay introduced BARKASS JUICE as a fresh and healthy choice for all age groups.

The idea for BARKASS JUICE was sparked during Bishal’s studies in Sadiya College, where he observed the increasing demand for pure and natural beverages among students and professionals alike. Determined to bridge this gap, he collaborated with experts in agriculture and nutrition to create recipes that are both delicious and packed with health benefits.

Since its inception, BARKASS has grown significantly, earning the trust of customers across India. With a focus on sustainability, the company uses eco-friendly packaging and supports local farmers by sourcing fresh fruits directly from them. The core values of **quality, innovation, and integrity** have been at the heart of BARKASS’s success.

Today, BARKASS offers a wide range of juice flavors, including tropical blends, classic favorites, and unique fusions. The brand continues to expand its reach, introducing new products and entering international markets. BARKASS is more than just a juice company—it’s a movement towards healthier living.
